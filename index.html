Comparative & Superlative Adjectives

We use comparisons to show differences between things.

Comparison Structures

  • as...as: She is as tall as her brother.
  • not as...as: This test is not as difficult as the last one.
  • less / least: This movie is less interesting.
  • more and more: English is becoming more and more important.
  • the more...the more: The more you study, the more you learn.
